Our App

Puro was designed for you. It’s a platform to help connect you to your property and career goals. With Puro, you can easily manage your cleaning needs on the go. List a property, a date, and a cleaner will pick it up. No need for you to call in to a housekeeping company, we keep it easy with just a few clicks for all properties. If you’re not looking for a clean, but looking to clean, Puro gives you the ability to display yourself as a cleaner and pick up properties whenever you like. Giving you the chance to build up clientele and make some extra cash.


Choosing a Role

When you first use the Cleaning House mobile application, you will be asked to choose a role. The available roles are Cleaner and Host. Cleaners are people who wish to earn money by cleaning properties posted on the application. Hosts are property owners who are willing to pay a Cleaner to clean one or more of their properties. Once you have chosen your role, the application will remember your choice in the future.


Managing Properties (Host)

As a Host, you will be able to add, view, and remove properties that you have posted. By navigating to the Host's Homescreen, a Host can view all of their properties by clicking the "Properties" button. From the properties screen, the Host can add more properties to the list by pressing the "Add Property" button. Then the Host can enter the property's information and add it to the current listings available to cleaners.


Browsing Properties (Cleaner)

As a cleaner, you will be able to browse the various properties that have been posted by Hosts and can view the wage, square footage, and address of each property. A Cleaner can view listed properties by navigating to the Cleaner's homescreen. From there the Cleaner can press the "Current Listings" button. A Cleaner can scroll throught the listed properties and select one they wish to clean.

Managing Schedule

Both Hosts and Cleaners have the ability to manage their own schedules. The Cleaner's schedule will display the days and times of the cleanings the Cleaner has accepted. The Host's schedule will display the days and times of the properties that are scheduled to be cleaned.
